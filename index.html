About ICES

Building the future of civil engineering in Malawi

Our Mission

To foster innovation and excellence in civil engineering while promoting sustainable development and professional growth among our members.

Our Vision

To be the leading organization in advancing civil engineering practices and solutions that shape a better future for Malawi.

Our Objectives

To provide a platform for knowledge sharing, professional development, and implementation of innovative engineering solutions.

Our Ethics

Upholding integrity, professionalism, and environmental responsibility in all our engineering practices.
